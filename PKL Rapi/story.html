Our History



Batu City is an Apple city with more than 50,000 tons of apples produced annually. Geographically, Batu city is located at an altitude between 700-2000 meters above sea level (masl), with an average temperature of 11-19 degrees Celsius. With such a climate, the air is quite cool and also has extraordinary soil fertility.

Precisely in 1929, the Netherlands has seriously developed the cultivation of apples. At that time the natives were employed in the Dutch apple orchard until Batu - Malang apple was able to dominate the apple market in Europe. We have started planting apple trees in the family garden (Novoadi) since 1975.
